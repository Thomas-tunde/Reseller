About the Reseller Partnership

Our Reseller Partnership Program is designed to expand access to Pluto Cloud’s enterprise-grade cloud storage and connectivity solutions. As an authorized reseller, you’ll empower small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) with scalable, secure, and affordable cloud infrastructure tailored to their needs.

We’re looking for partners in IT, telecommunications, and technology service sectors to join us in driving digital transformation across Africa. Leverage our robust platform to grow your business and enhance your service offerings.

Eligibility Criteria

We seek partners who:

  • Operate within the IT, telecommunications, or technology service sector in Nigeria or beyond.
  • Have an existing client base or proven experience managing enterprise or SME customers.
  • Demonstrate the capacity to promote and support cloud storage and connectivity solutions effectively.

Benefits of Partnering with Pluto Cloud

Enterprise-Grade Solutions

Access our secure, scalable cloud storage and connectivity services tailored for SMEs.

Competitive Pricing

White-Labeling Options

Scalable Infrastructure

Provide SMEs with flexible cloud solutions that grow with their business needs.

NDPR Compliance

Offer cloud services that meet Nigeria Data Protection Regulation standards.

How the Reseller Program Works

  1. Apply: Submit your proposal detailing your company background, client base, and reseller strategy.
  2. Onboard: Gain access to our reseller portal, training, and technical resources.
  3. Promote: Market Pluto Cloud’s storage and connectivity solutions to your SME clients.
  4. Earn: Enjoy competitive margins, track earnings, and grow with our support.
